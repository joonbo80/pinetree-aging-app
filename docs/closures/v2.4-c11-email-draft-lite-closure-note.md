## 1. Status

C11 Email Draft Lite is closed.

## 2. Purpose

C10 captured promise context. C11 turns the current party workflow
context into a safe draft email that a collector can review and send
manually.

This keeps the app useful for daily collection work without introducing
automatic outbound communication risk.

## 3. Implementation Summary

C11 adds an Email Draft area to the expanded Owner/Memo/Promise panel.

The draft uses:

- Party name.
- Currency and direction.
- Open amount.
- Invoice count.
- Oldest aging days.
- Statement status.
- Promise values when present.
- Memo text when present.

## 4. User Actions

The UI supports:

```text
Copy Draft
Open Email Draft
```

`Copy Draft` copies subject and body to the clipboard.

`Open Email Draft` opens a `mailto:` draft with an empty recipient so the
collector must still review, choose the recipient, and send manually.

## 5. Safety Decision

C11 intentionally does not use Microsoft Graph `Mail.Send`.

Reasons:

- Avoid accidental sending.
- Avoid adding mail permissions before review UX is mature.
- Keep audit expectations simple.
- Preserve the Workbench as a decision tool, not an automated email
  sender.

3. Confirm Email Draft section appears.
4. Confirm subject references party/currency/direction.
5. Confirm body includes balance, invoice count, aging, statement status,
   promise, and memo context.
6. Click Copy Draft.
7. Click Open Email Draft and verify a mail client draft opens.

## 7. Known Boundaries

C11 does not solve:

- Recipient email discovery.
- Contact list management.
- Email send history.
- Attachment generation.
- Outlook Graph integration.
- `Mail.Send` approval and audit.
