# v2.4 C11 Email Draft Lite Micro-spec

Date: 2026-05-15
Status: CLOSED
Parent: `docs/specs/v2.4-phase3-persistence-roadmap.md`
Previous step: `docs/closures/v2.4-c10-promise-tracking-lite-closure-note.md`

## 1. Goal

C11 adds a safe email draft helper to the Statement Collection
Workbench.

The goal is to help collectors turn party, balance, memo, and promise
context into a reusable email draft without sending email automatically.

## 2. Scope

C11 adds an Email Draft area inside the expanded workflow panel.

The draft includes:

- Party name.
- Currency.
- Direction.
- Open amount.
- Invoice count.
- Oldest aging days.
- Statement status.
- Promise date/amount/status when present.
- Internal memo context when present.

## 3. Actions

C11 provides two safe actions:

```text
Copy Draft
Open Email Draft
```

`Copy Draft` copies subject and body to the clipboard.

`Open Email Draft` uses `mailto:` with an empty recipient. The collector
must still choose the recipient and send manually.

## 4. Non-goals

C11 does not include:

- Microsoft Graph `Mail.Send`.
- Automatic email sending.
- Recipient lookup.
- Contact management.
- Email history.
- Attachment generation.
- Outlook add-in integration.

## 5. Safety Rule

C11 never sends email automatically.

Every outbound message requires collector review in the user's mail
client or manual copy/paste.

## 6. Completion Criteria

C11 is complete when:

- App build passes.
- Expanded party rows show an Email Draft section.
- Draft content includes party/balance/promise/memo context.
- Copy Draft works where clipboard access is available.
- Open Email Draft opens a mailto draft.
- No new Graph permissions are required.
